Getting Help

If you are recently diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes, there are several online support groups that can be a great resource to get support from people who understand what you are going through. These online chats are located at:
childrenwithdiabetes.com


If you are a parent of a child who has been diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes, there the American Diabetes Association have several support groups and educational programs that are specific to help you through this process. To find your nearest support group you can call:
1-800-DIABETES (800-342-2383) Monday-Friday,
8:30 a.m. to 8:00 p.m. ET
Or visit:
www.diabetes.org/about-us/contact-us


Remember, you or your child are not alone and there are many resources available that can help through this journey.
